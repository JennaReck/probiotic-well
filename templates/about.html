About Us

Probiotic Well is the culmination of years of my own personal experience with health and gastrointestinal issues and subsequent interest and research of probiotics. As the creator of Probiotic Well, I don't claim to have a doctorate of medicine or to be an unquestionable expert on the topic of gut health and probiotics. What I do have is my own personal experience with various probiotics in their many delivery methods and forms along with my own research and reading on the subject.

As a naturally skeptical person, I always liked to do my own research on natural 'cures' and supplements after traditional medicine failed to offer me much help. The most important things I learned were:

1. If it's too good to be true, it probably is.

Probiotics aren't magical cures. You probably won't find relief from your health issues overnight, nor will probiotics help all health concerns. If a probiotic (or any supplement, really) claims it will cure your cancer, grow your hair back, and make you 20 years younger, you should probably steer clear. Such promises abound in the alternative medicine and supplement market. Is that to say all supplements or probiotics are overrated and ineffective? No, of course not, which brings me to number two.

2. Not all probiotics are created equal.

When I first tried a few different probiotic types many years ago for my gastrointestinal issues, I didn't find any improvement and pretty much discounted all probiotics. This wasn't a fair assumption to make, however. Years later (and after much more research on probiotics and the science behind them) I gave them another shot, determined to find more effective and reputable ones.

I learned of many reasons why my prior experimentation with various probiotics didn't have much, if any, positive benefit. I hadn't taken into account CFU (bacteria) counts, the number of strains, whether or not they were coated to protect against stomach acid, or proper refrigerated storage. No wonder my low-CFU, 1-strain, non-stomach-acid-protected, improperly-stored probiotics weren't effective in the past!

Now I've found many which work better for me, and the knowledge I've learned along the way has given me some new hope for my personal gut issues. Probiotic Well is my way of trying to give back what I've learned; a place to offer honest probiotic reviews and informative articles to help others make better informed choices. After all, "better health begins with better knowledge," and I can only hope this site can accomplish that.
